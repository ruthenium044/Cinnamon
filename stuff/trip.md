## **Paphos / Coral Bay (Days 1–4)**

* Calm, shallow waters – ideal for PPB and Nitrox courses
* Beginner-friendly wrecks: Vera K, Amphorae Caves, St George’s Island
* Sightseeing: Paphos Archaeological Park, Tombs of the Kings, Coral Bay beach
* Compact harbor, walkable dive shops

### **1. Peak Performance Buoyancy (PPB)**

* Focus: Perfect your trim, weighting, and finning.
* Benefit: More control, less air consumption, safer around wrecks and reefs.
* Location: Paphos

### **2. Nitrox (Enriched Air Diver)**

* Focus: Use oxygen-enriched air to extend bottom time.
* Benefit: Less fatigue, longer dives, safer repetitive diving.
* Location: Paphos

## **Larnaca (Days 5–10)**

* Zenobia wreck dives, AOW courses, night or cavern dives
* Nearby shallow wrecks and reefs for fun dives
* Sightseeing: Hala Sultan Tekke, Salt Lake, Larnaca Old Town
* Close to airport, multiple dive centers, one hotel sufficient

### **3. Advanced Open Water (AOW)**

* Focus: Extend depth to 30 m, improve navigation skills.
* Required dives: Deep + Navigation
* Elective dives: Wreck + Night
* Benefit: Access to deeper wrecks like Zenobia, night diving, more confident diving.
* Location: Larnaca

#### **Specialties**

* Night Dive – skill-building in low light, improves buoyancy and situational awareness.
* Wreck Adventure – guided wreck dives (exterior, superstructure, no penetration).
